What's Included in a Smile Makeover?

A smile makeover at Astra is not a one-size-fits-all package. It's a bespoke combination of treatments selected specifically for your teeth, face, and goals. Depending on your needs, your makeover may include:

Porcelain veneers to reshape and brighten your front teeth. Professional whitening to ensure a uniform, radiant shade. Dental crowns to restore damaged teeth. Invisalign to correct alignment before cosmetic work. Gum contouring to create a balanced, symmetrical smile line.

Every element is chosen and designed to work together in harmony — because a truly great smile is about the complete picture, not just individual teeth.

Who Is It For?

Our smile makeover patients come from all walks of life. You might be considering a makeover if you:

Feel self-conscious about your smile in photos or social situations. Have chipped, stained, or worn teeth that age your appearance. Want to look your best for a wedding, career change, or fresh start. Simply believe you deserve a smile that matches how you feel inside.

There's no wrong reason to want a better smile. And at Astra, there's no judgment — only expertise, care, and outstanding results.
